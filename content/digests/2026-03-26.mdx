**TL;DR.** Run cold email for volume and LinkedIn for quality as one Claude-coordinated system, not two parallel funnels.

**Takeaways**

- Stack: Instantly (150 inboxes/50 domains, ~3k emails/day → ~45k leads/mo) + HeyReach (5 accounts, ~2,400 accepted connections/mo) + Claude Code as the coordinator.
- Email sequences stay brutally short: 2 touches over 3 days, no tracking pixels, no unsub links that read like marketing — write like a human.
- LinkedIn's low ceiling is the feature, not the bug — its constraint forces ICP discipline and produces warmer, higher-intent replies.
- Claude scores every prospect 1–10 before assignment: 8–10 get email + LinkedIn, 5–7 email only, below 5 dropped.
