**TL;DR.** Demo your product as a 5-part story — pain, dream, bridge, proof, next steps — not a feature tour.

**Takeaways**

- Frame every demo around the customer's current pain and the future state they want, then position your product as the bridge between them.
- Back the narrative with real customer proof and close with explicit next steps — features without context don't stick.
- Stories drive ~2x better retention than feature walkthroughs.
