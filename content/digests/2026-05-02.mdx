**TL;DR.** A 21-year-old built an AI OnlyFans persona in 4 weeks that cleared $43K in 30 days using just 4 markdown files (persona, voice, flux, brain) wired into Claude Code, Flux, and ElevenLabs.

**Takeaways**

- The whole "engine" is 4 .md files: `persona.md` (backstory + voice rules), `flux.md` (LoRA + lighting seeds), `voice.md` (ElevenLabs clone), `brain.md` (per-subscriber JSON memory of names, spend, tip triggers, personal facts).
- Build timeline collapsed from 18 months (Aitana López) and 6 months (Emily Pellegrini) to 4 weeks — the moat isn't tech, it's character consistency: "If it sounds like Wikipedia, delete and restart."
- Voice rules are the unlock for "feels human": lowercase only, no periods, comma splices instead of question marks, max 3 emojis, "fr" capped at 3x/day.
- Net ~$32,710 after platform/processing fees, and the author claims the same 4-file pattern ports to Instagram/TikTok personas — bottleneck is now taste, not tooling.
