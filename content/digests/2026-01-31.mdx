**TL;DR.** GTM Engineers vs SDRs isn't a debate — automate outbound below ~250-500 accounts per rep, go human-in-the-loop above it.

**Takeaways**

- The inflection point: SDRs win on quality for focused account lists; GTMEs win on coverage once you need to work 250-500+ accounts per rep.
- Use GTMEs when market penetration is low (early-stage, lots of targets, small budget) or ACV is $10-25K and can't justify SDR CAC — they can hit 5,000+ accounts with tailored automation.
- Quick math: 5,000 accounts × 1% conversion × $15K ACV = $750K pipeline from one $150K GTME (5:1), vs. two SDRs covering only 1,000 accounts on the same budget.
