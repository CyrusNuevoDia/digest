**TL;DR.** The pull is the quiet obsession you can't argue your way out of — and ignoring it costs you years of "I saw that first.".

**Takeaways**

- The pull isn't FOMO or impulse — it lingers for months or years and keeps surfacing even when you actively try to kill it.
- Stability feels like the loving answer; family and friends will reinforce waiting. The "right time" never arrives.
- If the gap between what you see and what everyone else sees feels glaring, that's the signal — act, don't wait for permission.
- "Nobody is ever haunted by the things they tried."
