**TL;DR.** One focused hour a day beats grinding 12—clarity and recovery, not raw time, are what unlock extreme output.

**Takeaways**

- Three modes of work: Building (deep bursts), Maintenance (systemized upkeep), Recovery (the rest most people skip but where breakthroughs form).
- Successful creatives "work in their mind" — they think, plot, and scheme constantly, then execute fast once clarity hits.
- Attention is your RAM; every open tab and obligation degrades performance. Protect it ruthlessly.
- 365 hours (one hour a day for a year) on one meaningful project is enough to change your life — stop waiting for more time.
