**TL;DR.** The real AI threat isn't that it fails — it's that it works, and the political backlash is already being built while builders stay naively silent.

**Takeaways**

- "The political risk is not that ai fails. It is that ai works" — once AI becomes the convenient explanation for why life feels harder, accurate causality stops mattering.
- Social media is the cautionary tale: builders believed usefulness and good intentions would protect them. They were wrong.
- Neutrality is a losing strategy. The only defense is narrative power, coalitions, and a clearly articulated mission — "not as aspiration, but as armor."
- Incumbents, politicians, and activist institutions are already organizing the anti-AI narrative now, before most people have formed opinions.
