**TL;DR.** Sell your solution by quantifying the gap between today's painful current state and your delivered outcomes.

**Takeaways**

- Frame current state in hard numbers: labor cost, error rate, time delays.
- Mirror the same metrics in your solution (e.g., 80% labor cut, 95% fewer errors, same-day delivery).
- The pitch is the delta — make it concrete, not abstract.

**TL;DR.** Four discovery-call questions that cut through fluff: what hooked you, why is it broken, why now, and show me where it hurts.

**Takeaways**

- "Why now?" is the killer — without urgency, deals stall indefinitely.
- Asking buyers to walk through their current process surfaces real pain (and hidden incumbents) faster than feature pitches.
- Leading with "what made you interested?" anchors the call to their motivation, not your script.
