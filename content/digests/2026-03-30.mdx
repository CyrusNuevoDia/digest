**TL;DR.** LinkedIn lead gen is a psychology game, not a volume game — convert people by accelerating trust, not by blasting more DMs.

**Takeaways**

- Every offer has a "trust threshold"; high-ticket retainers need massive trust before a prospect will even take a call, so cold pitches to strangers fail by default.
- Three trust accelerators: demonstrated expertise (show frameworks/results, don't claim them), consistent presence (mere exposure effect — week 1 "who?" → week 12 "I should talk to them"), and social proof (borrowed trust via case studies and testimonials).
- Reframe: content isn't for reach, it's for repeated exposure that compounds familiarity into conversion.
