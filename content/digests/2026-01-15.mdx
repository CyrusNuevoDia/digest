**TL;DR.** Stop trying to invent brilliance on the spot — develop 8-10 core ideas you've refined deeply, and learn to connect them to anything.

**Takeaways**

- Build an "inner album of greatest hits": 8-10 big ideas you've thought through hundreds of times, ready to deploy in any conversation.
- Sounding articulate isn't about memorizing books or being entertaining — it's about having a small set of well-worn ideas you can pattern-match to new topics.
- The fear of "repeating yourself" is the trap; your best ideas are exactly what new audiences need to hear.
