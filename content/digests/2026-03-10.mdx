**Takeaways**

- Every prospect's "yes" fires unpredictably (maybe once a year), so cadence beats intensity: contact monthly, mixing light (email), medium (in-person), heavy (events/gifts).

- Memorable gifts compound: TrialPay sent customers cacti in branded pots, and Arampell saw those pots in offices for years afterward.
- Founders build awareness by becoming the press/analyst source of truth in their category, not by pitching the CEO directly.
