**TL;DR.** Stack Paperclip + gstack + autoresearch to spin up a one-person AI company with an engineering team and overnight R&D lab in under 10 minutes.

**Takeaways**

- Paperclip = the company dashboard (mission, agents, budgets); gstack (Garry Tan) = 15 specialist eng roles like CEO/CTO/QA; autoresearch (Karpathy) = autonomous overnight experiments.
- Install flow is dead simple: `npx paperclipai onboard --yes`, then `git clone gstack` into `~/.claude/skills/`.
- Killer gstack commands: `/office-hours` (turns rough ideas into plans), `/qa` (opens a real browser, finds bugs, writes regression tests), `/ship` (test → push → PR in one command).
- Follow-up to a 2.7M-view post — written to answer "cool demo, but how does it actually work?"
