**TL;DR.** LinkedIn growth in 2026 is won by personal profiles posting PDF carousels with hooks that deliver value upfront, not bait.

**Takeaways**

- Ditch the company page — personal profiles get ~20x the organic reach because LinkedIn rewards humans talking to humans.
- Pick ONE lane in your headline; vague "CEO | Speaker | Dad | Coffee" combos kill algorithmic matching and lose leads in 3 seconds.
- PDF carousels win because 45-60s dwell time signals "keep promoting this" — hook slide, 7-9 value slides, clean CTA.
- Lead hooks with the value itself ("Founders waste 80% of their LinkedIn effort…"), not tired curiosity bait like "I almost quit last year."
